# Experience Product Notes

## Mission

Recognizing the people who create extraordinary movie experiences.

## Product Promise

Recognition should be easier to capture than to overlook.

## Product Frame

Experience is an Employee Experience Platform for Celebration Cinema North. It is not a single recognition campaign. Seasonal activations, skins, cards, and reward catalogs can change, but the platform language stays stable.

## Frozen Language

- Product name: **Experience**
- Platform category: **Employee Experience Platform**
- Employee side: **Employee Experience**
- Manager side: **Leadership Experience**
- Employee recognition unit: **Experience Moment**
- Employee paper workflow: **Experience Card**
- Employee reward currency: **Miles**
- Manager recognition unit: **Leadership Recognition**

## Role Experiences

- Employee Experience: employees view Miles, recent Experience Moments, rewards, community progress, and their profile.
- Leadership Experience: managers view leadership health, coverage, coaching insights, leadership recognition, leadership rewards, and employees awaiting recognition.
- Admin/GM Experience: admins configure employees, recognitions, excellence checks, rewards, skins, display settings, and seasonal activations.

## Leadership Rules

- Managers do not earn employee XP.
- Managers do not earn spendable employee Miles.
- Managers receive Leadership Recognition for coaching, communication, coverage, and operational impact.
- Leadership Rewards are not purchased through the employee Trading Post.

## First Seasonal Activation: The Odyssey

- Management Preview: **July 9, 2026**
- Employee Launch: **July 16, 2026**
- Community Goal: **15,700 Miles**
- Core phrase: **Every Mile Matters**
- Meaning: Every Mile represents a Moment That Mattered.

## Core Rules

- Employees do not self-submit Miles.
- Managers verify and recognize employee moments.
- Recognition matters more than competition.
- Miles are earned through manager-recognized employee behaviors that improve the building, the team, and the guest experience.

## Primary Workflows

- Manager captures an Experience Moment in under 10 seconds.
- Manager enters turned-in Experience Card checklist items at the end of the shift.
- Admin/manager bulk assigns and prints daily Experience Cards by employee and scheduled area.
- Employee views Miles, recent moments, rewards, and community progress.
- Manager uses Leadership Experience to monitor coverage and coaching needs.
- Admin manages recognition types, excellence checks, reward inventory, seasonal settings, skins, and launch readiness.
- TV mode celebrates community progress, spotlight moments, recognition leaderboard, department progress, rotating rewards, and countdown.
